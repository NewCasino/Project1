2. Menu sections

Sports

 Sports page consists of the following sections:

  1. Next Events displays the next betting events available, split by sport. Can be filtered for pre-defined time frame (2h, 4h,…48h) from the moment when checked and by bet type.
  2. Live Today shows upcoming sports events from that day included in betting offer.
  3. Live Now lists the matches that are in-play. The menu has the events sorted by sport, country, competition and event. Customers can find updated Home/Away or Home/Draw/Away odds even in the menu.
  4. All Odds provides the non-live betting offer, sorted by sport, country, competition and event. Similar to <Live Now> customers can find odds even in the menu.
  5. Banner slider shows information about current and future sports promotions.
  6. Live Overview consists of the same selection of in-play events as Live Now.
  7. Popular Events
  8. Mini games, displayed just for logged in players, is a cross-selling spot for casino mini games.
  9. Betting Slip
  10. My Sports Account section for logged in players show their balance, provide access to Betting History and to Help & Support and offer possibility to set time and odds format. Not logged in players can access just Help & Support and set time and odds format. Help & Support page provide relevant details, rules and conditions for sports betting, answers to common questions and is structured with the following menu: Betting Rules; How to place a bet; Canceling a bet; Bet Confirmation; Responsible gaming; T&C; FAQ; Active Promotions.

Virtual Sports

This section is split into two similar categories: Horse Racing and Greyhounds, both offering the possibility to watch the virtual races in the browser. Below the screen, current race and future ones are listed and the corresponding list of participants for each race, their form and the odds offered if they win or finish in top 3 (Horse Racing) or top 2 (Greyhounds).
