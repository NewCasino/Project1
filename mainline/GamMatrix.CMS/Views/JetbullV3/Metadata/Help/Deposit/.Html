1. How do I make a deposit to my Jetbull account?

    Click the 'Deposit' button, available in the top menu of any Jetbull.com page. Please note that you need to be logged in to make a deposit.

     

  2. How long does it take for my deposit to get approved?

    Almost all deposits are approved instantly. An exception is a bank transfer, that can take several business days as it needs to be processed by your bank before we receive the funds. Once we receive your funds, we will credit your [Metadata:htmlencode(/Metadata/Settings.Operator_DisplayName)] account.

     

  3. Which deposit methods do you have available?

    We have a very large selection of deposit methods! Please click 'Payment' button, in the top menu of any [Metadata:htmlencode(/Metadata/Settings.Operator_DisplayName)] page to see all of the payment methods available to you. Available methods will depend on which country you live in.

     

  4. Which credit/debit cards do you accept?

    [Metadata:htmlencode(/Metadata/Settings.Operator_DisplayName)] accepts all Visa and Mastercard/Maestro Credit and Debit cards.

     

  5. Will I be charged any fees when depositing into my [Metadata:htmlencode(/Metadata/Settings.Operator_DisplayName)] account?

    There are no fees for depositing into your [Metadata:htmlencode(/Metadata/Settings.Operator_DisplayName)] account, unless otherwise is specified on Deposit page.

     

  6. What is the minimum/maximum amounts that I can deposit at a time?

    The minimum deposit amount is €10 or equivalent in your currency. The maximum deposit amount vary between the different deposit methods you can choose between.

     

  7. Is there a way for me to set daily, weekly and monthly deposit limits?

    Yes, you can set Daily, Weekly or Monthly deposit limits. These  can be set by accessing the My Account section when you are logged in. Limits can be amended at anytime in the same section.

     

  8. How do I transfer money from my bank account?

    From the Deposit page, select Bank Transfer, and follow the instructions on the screen.
