1. How can I make a withdrawal from my account?

    You can make a withdrawal whenever you like, provided the funds you try to withdraw is not a bonus that has requirement that has yet not been met. You can also withdraw as many times as you wish, but note that we cannot process a withdrawal less than 24 hours after your last deposit, due to the anti money laundering and fraud prevention laws that [Metadata:htmlencode(/Metadata/Settings.Operator_DisplayName)] adhere to.

  2. Are there any limits on how much I can withdraw?

    The minimum withdrawal amount is €10.00 or currency equivalent. The maximum withdrawal amount is that can be processed per day is €5000, or currency equivalent.

  3. Can I cancel a withdrawal request?

    Yes, you can cancel a withdrawal request by accessing 'Pending Withdrawals' section, under 'My Account' and following the instructions on that page.

  4. Can I get my winnings paid back to my credit/debit card?

    Yes, you can as long as it is the same card that you used to make your original deposit and the issuing bank accepts transactions to the card.

  5. I used my credit/debit card to deposit, can I request a payment by using any other method?

    Yes you can but, we may need full documentation from you and details of your new method.

  6. Why can’t I choose freely among all withdrawal methods offered when I request a withdrawal?

    This is due to anti money laundering regulations that we adhere to and also for your security. Unless authorised, a withdrawal must be sent to the same payment method used to deposit.

  7. Do you charge any withdrawal fees?      

    As a Jetbull customer you always get the first withdrawal per month free of charge. The following withdrawals the same month is charged with a 1% fee, which is deducted from the withdrawal amount.

    Please note that we reserve the right to charge a fee equal to our own costs, including the cost of the deposits, for withdrawals of funds without a turnover that is equal or more than the amount of funds deposited.

  8. How long do withdrawals take to process?

    This can vary depending on your chosen withdrawal method. Please check 'Payment Methods' to see all of the withdrawal methods available to you and their processing times.

  9. Why is it that a withdrawal to my card takes days while a deposit is immediate?

    We have a number of controls and checks that take place before any withdrawal leaves [Metadata:htmlencode(/Metadata/Settings.Operator_DisplayName)] and this delays withdrawals by approximately 12 hours. These checks are part of our ongoing commitment to maintaining the security of our customers' funds. Any additional delay would be on the payment provider side.

  10. What happens if the card I had withdrawn money to has expired or been cancelled?

    You will need to let us know of any changes of card and supply proof of being out of date or reason for the change. Any new card will have to be registered with us and onto your [Metadata:htmlencode(/Metadata/Settings.Operator_DisplayName)] account.

  11. Can I make a withdrawal from my account and have it sent to someone else?

    No, this is deemed as a third party transaction and will not be permitted under any circumstances.
