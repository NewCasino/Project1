  9. What is an IBAN?

    IBAN stands for International Bank Account Number. IBAN is used in international bank wire transactions and makes withdrawals of your winnings easier.

  10. What is a CVC2 code?

    CVC2 is an acronym for "Card Verification Code". This code is required as a security measure when making international telephone purchases and internet payments by credit card. This code consists of three digits and can be found on the back of your credit card.
